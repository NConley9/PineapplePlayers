# Pineapple Players — Game Rules & Card Decks

## Overview
Adult party card game for in-person groups. Players take turns drawing, playing cards, and doing the action listed.

## Card Types
- **Dare** — Active player must perform an action.
- **Challenge** — Active player competes with or involves another player; group votes on winner/loser where noted.
- **Group** — All players participate.

## Turn Mechanics
1. Active player draws two cards and chooses one to play.
2. The chosen card is read aloud to all players.
3. Active player performs the action (or passes — see Pass Punishment below).
4. Turn ends; advance to the next player in order.

## "Another Player" Rule
Whenever a card says "another player," it means a player of the opposite sex who is **not** that player's significant other. If no such player is available, the active player may choose any other player.

## Pass Punishment
A player may pass on any card rather than perform it. Passing is not free — the player must **remove one article of clothing** as their penalty. The same penalty applies to **losing any challenge**. Track this; the social pressure is part of the game.
